Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 15, 2026

Ship100 ("the App") is a 100-day design and vibecoding challenge tracker built by Siddhant. This policy explains how the App handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

Ship100 does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All app data is stored locally on your device using on-device storage (AsyncStorage).

This includes:

  • Your challenge progress (days completed, proof links)
  • App preferences and settings

2. Data That Stays on Your Device

The App operates entirely offline. Your progress data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it (e.g., using the share feature to export a completion card image).

3. Third-Party Services

The App is built with Expo / React Native. Expo may collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostics as described in Expo's Privacy Policy. No additional third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are used.

4. Permissions

The App may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage / Media – to save and share completion card images you generate.

Permissions are only requested when needed and can be revoked at any time through your device settings.

5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13. Since no personal data is collected from any user, the App is safe for all ages.

6. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all App data at any time by:

  • Clearing the app's data from your device settings, or
  • Uninstalling the App

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
